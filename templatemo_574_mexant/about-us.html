Engineering Resilience

Military-Grade Cyber Defense

Led by Executive Director Brian Grant and COO Donali Grant, AESURUS was forged with a singular mission: to protect enterprise data from an increasingly hostile digital landscape.

We do not rely on passive firewalls. We utilize active threat hunting, Next-Gen Antivirus (NGAV), and continuous Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) powered by Sophos.

Sovereign Cloud Architecture

True resilience requires infrastructure that cannot be compromised. We architect robust, multi-cloud environments utilizing Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI).

Our custom deployments guarantee zero operational downtime and lightning-fast automated failovers via Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS).

Uncompromising Compliance

Regulatory frameworks are not optional. We provide end-to-end technical consulting to align your infrastructure seamlessly with the Jamaica Data Protection Act (JDPA).

Through rigorous penetration testing and routine vulnerability assessments, we ensure your enterprise remains both secure and legally compliant.
